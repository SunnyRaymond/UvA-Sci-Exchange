One way to denote the payoff matrix of a game is this:
  \[
      \begin{bNiceMatrix}[first-row,first-col]
          &  L 	& R\\
        T & (2,-4) & (6, 3) \\
        B & (1, 2) & (8,1) 
      \end{bNiceMatrix}			
  \]     

Another way to visualize a normal-form game is this:
    \begin{center}
        \nfgame{T B L R 
        $2$ $6$ $1$ $8$ 
        $-4$ $3$ $2$ $1$}       
    \end{center}
